Introducing Emojify

Spruce up vapid, stale text with a little more emotion. All you have to do is enter content below, press the Emojify button, and receive your playful new text.

Why Emoji?

It's a scientific fact that humans read text containing emoji keywords at an average rate of 300x faster than typical blocks of text.* This means that emoji are well-suited for advertisements, novels, and even obituaries!

Here are a few other use cases:

  • Writing a university paper with a strict word count? Emojify!
  • Trying to develop a website with an intial load of less than 10k? Emojify!
